World

Sky High

Overview

Sky High is the first zone in MOAB Adventure. The player will spawn in here once all options have been chosen, and will begin playing the game.

Appearance

Sky High is a zone that takes place above an ocean with dark blue water. Islands and clouds are supposed to render, but they don't show up in-game.

Spawning

Wooden boxes, as the basic enemy of MOAB Adventure, spawn frequently here.
Metal boxes spawn here too, at about 1/4 of the rate.

Strategy

The main enemy, wooden boxes, are quite weak, so most strategies will work against them. They appear in large amounts, so it's mostly best to employ multi-target weaponry, such as Bomb Shooter to clear the crowds.

As for the metal boxes, single-target methods are more effective, as they are more spaced out.
